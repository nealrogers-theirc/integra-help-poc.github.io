Business Process Overview

High Level Business Process Flow

Purchasing Approvals

A D4 owner (HQ, RAI) or D5 owner (CRRD) receives a purchase requisition (PR) to approve. Once approved, the PR becomes a purchase order (PO) in Draft status in the system. The requestor then submits the PO up the supervisory hierarchy for approval based on the dollar spend.

Before approving a purchasing document, the following details must be reviewed for each line item:

  • Financial dimensions are correct
  • Quantity, price, currency, vendor, etc., are correct
  • The purchase is valid
  • There is a justifiable business reason for the purchase

PO Approvals

  • Requestor submits draft PO to Workflow which is sent to the immediate supervisor
  • If the immediate supervisor has the appropriate authority level, the PO remains in that person’s queue for approval
  • If spend is higher than immediate supervisor’s approval authority, once the immediate supervisor approves it, the PO moves up the hierarchy until the right authority level is reached
  • Every approver must review and mark the PO Complete, one person at a time.
  • Once approved, the status of the PO changes to Approved and returns to the requestor for confirmation who then sends it to the vendor as a confirmed PO for execution.
  • Please note, a PO is not considered a binding document until it has been confirmed, printed, and sent to the vendor.

Approve a Purchase Requisition

Purpose

Use this procedure to approve a purchase requisition (PR) submitted by a requestor.

Note: For PRs created for capitalized fixed assets, the approver must apply the applicable asset group to the PR line, e.g., ICONSTRUCT, IVEHICLES, IEQUIPFURNI, etc. This value is required for the subsequent creation of the fixed asset record. This field is located in the Line details section of the PR, under the Fixed assets tab.

Prerequisites

  • A completed PR has been submitted for approval

Steps

  1. Navigate modules to Common > Work items > Work items assigned to me.

  1. Alternate navigation path: On the Integra home page, look under Work items assigned to me, and select one of the Please review blue hyperlinks.

  1. In the list, click the link to review the purchase requisition requiring review.

  1. Scroll down to the Purchase requisition lines to review each product, quantity, price, vendor account and name, and financial dimensions (financial dimensions are listed in the Account field of the PR line).

  1. Click Workflow to open the drop-down dialog and click Complete.

  1. In the Purchase requisition review with D4 approvers - Complete window, add a comment to acknowledge the approval of this purchase requisition and click Complete.

Result

The purchase requisition has been approved and a draft PO has been generated.

Reject a Purchase Requisition

Purpose

To reject a purchase requisition and return to the requestor

Prerequisites

  • A completed PR submitted for approval

Steps

  1. On the dashboard page, look for the Work items assigned to me, and select one of the Please review blue hyperlinks.

  1. In the list, click the purchase requisition you wish to review.

  1. Review the PR.
  2. Click Workflow to open the drop-down dialog then click Reject.

  1. In the Purchase requisition review with D4 approvers - Reject window, provide a reason for rejecting the purchase requisition and click Reject.

Result

The purchase requisition has rejected.

The requestor can make changes to the PR and submit to workflow again or delete the PR if it is not required.

Delegate Approval for a Purchase Requisition

Purpose

Use this procedure to delegate authority to another person to review and approve purchasing documents.

Prerequisites

  • A completed PR has been submitted for approval

Steps

  1. On the dashboard page, look for the Work items assigned to me, and select one of the Please review blue hyperlinks.
  2. In the list, select the purchase requisition you wish to delegate, click on Workflow in the Action Pane, then click on Delegate.
  3. In Delegate window, search the delegate name by typing the first name under the Name field then click Apply.
  4. Please note, delegating this specific approval does not mean you are delegating other responsibilities, it only pertains to this specific purchase requisition.

  1. Click on the delegate name to confirm and click Delegate.

Result

The purchasing document was sent to the delegate for approval.

Approve or Reject a Purchase Order

Purpose

To approve or reject a purchase order (PO)

Prerequisites

  • PO has been submitted for approval.

Steps

  1. On the dashboard page, look for the Work items assigned to me and select the Authorization Matrix Signature blue hyperlink.

  1. In the list, select the purchase order you wish to review and potentially approve by marking it with a check √ mark on the left, then click Open.

  1. Review the following details on the PO prior to approving.
    • Financial dimensions (D-codes)
    • Item, quantity, vendor, etc.
  2. You have performed your due diligence with respect to:
    • The purchase is valid
    • You can justify the purchase to the business
  3. Click Workflow to open the drop-down dialog and click Complete to approve. If you need to reject the PO, click Return.

  1. In the Purchase order workflow - Complete window, add a comment to acknowledge the approval of this purchase order and click Complete.
Note: To reject a PO, click Return.

Result

The PO was approved (or rejected).
