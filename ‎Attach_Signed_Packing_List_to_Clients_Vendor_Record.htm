Attach Signed Packing List to Client’s Vendor Record

Purpose

Use this procedure to attach the client-signed packing list to the client vendor record.

Prerequisites

  • Packing list has been printed.
  • Client has signed the hard copy of the packing list.

Steps

  1. From the Integra home page, navigate the modules Accounts Payable  Vendors  All vendors.
  2. Search for and select the client vendor record.

  1. In the top right corner of page, click the paper clip icon.

  2. On the menu at the top of page, click New and select File from the drop-down selection list.

  3. In the right-hand panel, click Browse.
  4. Locate and select relevant document, as per standard Windows browsing functionality. (It may take a few seconds for upload to complete)

  5. Validate the attachment is listed in the left-hand panel.
  6. Click X in the right-hand corner of page to close the window and return to the previous screen.

Result

You have uploaded (attached) a signed packing list to a client’s vendor record.
